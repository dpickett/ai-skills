name: architectural-interview
description: Interview the user about designing a piece of software
---

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this architectural question until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.

Ask me questions, one at a time, until you have enough context to put forth a plan.
